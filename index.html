Our Projects

Artificial Retina Project

Designed to restore vision for individuals with retinal degenerative diseases, such as retinitis pigmentosa. It typically consists of a small implant with an array of electrodes placed on or near the retina, a camera that captures visual information, and a processor that translates these images into electrical signals. These signals stimulate the remaining functional cells in the retina, allowing the user to perceive visual information and improve their vision. The system helps individuals regain partial sight and navigate their environment more effectively.

Micro-controller Projects

Involve creating systems that automate or control various functions based on sensor inputs and user commands.
